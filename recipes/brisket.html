Brisket Marinade!

Directions:

Combine wine, Worcestershire sauce
and liquid smoke in a medium bowl.
Stir in celery flakes, pepper, onion salt, and garlic salt.

    This is what you're going too need:

  • ½ cup red wine
  • ¼ cup Worcestershire sauce
  • ¼ cup liquid smoke flavoring
  • 4 teaspoons celery flakes
  • 4 teaspoons pepper
  • 2 teaspoons onion salt
  • 2 teaspoons garlic salt
